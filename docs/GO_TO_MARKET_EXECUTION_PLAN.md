# PropertyPilot AI — Go-To-Market Execution Plan

## 1) Positioning

**Category:** AI Revenue OS for real-estate agents and brokerages.  
**Promise:** More listings, faster follow-up, and predictable conversion from lead to appointment.  
**Primary ICP:**  
- Solo agents in USA/EU doing 5-30 transactions/year  
- Team leaders with 3-20 agents  
- Agencies in Middle East focused on premium inventory

## 2) Core Offer Stack

1. **Starter (€197/mo):** AI listing engine + lead score base + pro PDFs.  
2. **Pro (€497/mo):** CRM, automations, white-label PDF, advanced AI, voice calls.  
3. **Agency (€897/mo):** team workflows, unlimited voice, war room, multi-user operations.  
4. **One-shot Agency Boost (€2,497):** done-for-you setup + launch support.

## 3) Funnel Architecture

1. **Traffic:** SEO pages, YouTube tutorials, founder-led LinkedIn, paid Meta/Google tests.  
2. **Capture:** Industry-specific landing pages + lead magnet ("7-day listing acceleration playbook").  
3. **Activation:** Live onboarding + first listing generated in <10 minutes.  
4. **Conversion:** Trial-to-paid sprint with in-app nudges and scheduled success calls.  
5. **Expansion:** Team seats, white-label upgrades, Agency Boost upsell.

## 4) 90-Day Launch Execution

### Phase 1 (Days 1-30): Proof of Demand
- Launch 3 geo pages (US, EU, Middle East) with localized copy and case format.
- Publish 12 SEO articles targeting bottom-funnel terms:
  - "real estate AI listing generator"
  - "real estate lead follow up automation"
  - "AI CRM for real estate agents"
- Run 2 paid campaigns:
  - Meta lead ads (agent persona)
  - Google high-intent search terms
- Weekly KPI targets:
  - 1,500 qualified sessions
  - 120 trial signups
  - 15 paid conversions

### Phase 2 (Days 31-60): Conversion Optimization
- Add 3 conversion assets:
  - ROI calculator
  - interactive product demo
  - industry proof page (before/after listings)
- Run onboarding webinars 2x/week.
- Build email lifecycle:
  - Day 0: welcome + quick-start
  - Day 1: activation checklist
  - Day 3: case-study proof
  - Day 5: upgrade incentive
  - Day 8: last-call offer
- Weekly KPI targets:
  - Trial activation >55%
  - Trial-to-paid >18%
  - CAC payback <3 months

### Phase 3 (Days 61-90): Scale
- Partner channel with coaches, broker networks, and real-estate media newsletters.
- Referral loop:
  - Give 1 free month for each closed referral
  - Agency Boost discount for 3+ referrals
- Expand paid budget only on channels with:
  - CAC <= 35% of first-year gross margin
  - payback <= 90 days

## 5) Sales Script Framework

1. Diagnose volume and follow-up bottlenecks.  
2. Quantify missed revenue from slow response time.  
3. Demonstrate a live listing-to-follow-up flow in product.  
4. Offer a 14-day rollout plan with clear milestone ownership.  
5. Close with plan recommendation + onboarding calendar.

## 6) Weekly Operating Cadence

- **Monday:** Growth review (traffic, activation, conversion, retention).  
- **Tuesday:** Product + marketing sync on funnel bottlenecks.  
- **Wednesday:** Live demo webinar + objection handling updates.  
- **Thursday:** Performance creative refresh (ads + landing variants).  
- **Friday:** Revenue quality review (MRR, churn reasons, expansion signals).

## 7) North Star and Guardrails

- **North Star:** Net New MRR from activated users.  
- **Guardrails:**
  - Trial activation rate
  - CAC payback
  - 30-day retention
  - Refund/churn reason by acquisition channel
